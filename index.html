Activities at Lightway Ministry

We’re thrilled to have you join our community. To help you get involved, here’s a quick look at our weekly activities:

  • Sundays: Worship Service – Our main service starts at 8 AM.
  • Mondays: Victory Hour – A powerful time of prayer and spiritual breakthrough.
  • Wednesdays: Communion Service – Join us as we partake in the Lord’s Supper and worship together.
  • Thursdays: Youth Reset – A vibrant gathering for the youth to grow and connect.
  • Fridays: Prophetic [&] Deliverance Service – Experience prophetic ministry and deliverance.

ABOUT US

OUR VISION,OUR MISSION-TOGETHER IN PURPOSE

Our Community

Lightway Ministry is a community of believers focused on living purposefully and building strong character rooted in faith. Our mission is to guide and support each individual in their journey toward spiritual growth and fulfillment.

We are led by the loving and God-fearing couple, Pastor Pat and Pastor Regina Uduagbomen. Their leadership is marked by their dedication to serving both God and people, making them beloved figures within our community. At Lightway Ministry, we believe that every believer has a role to play in fulfilling God’s purpose, and we are here to walk that path together.
